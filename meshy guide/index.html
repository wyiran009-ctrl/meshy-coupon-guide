Exclusive Coupon Guide

Follow these 4 simple steps to activate your custom creator discount and unlock your Pro benefits.

1
Step 1

Remove the Default Offer

Currently, Meshy has a default 50% off promotion for all new users. To use your specific creator coupon, you must first click the "Remove Coupon" button at the bottom of the pricing page.

2
Step 2

Select Pro Monthly Plan

Once the default offer is removed, locate the Pro plan (designed for individual creators). Ensure the toggle is set to Billed monthly and click "Subscribe Now" on the Pro card.

3
Step 3

Enter Your Promo Code

On the checkout page, look at the subtotal section on the left. Click the button labeled "Add promotion code" and paste your unique coupon code into the field.

4
Step 4

Verify and Subscribe

After applying the code, the total amount will update instantly. Review the new total, then click "Subscribe" to unlock your Pro features and start creating.

Need Assistance?

If you encounter any issues during the checkout process, please contact the Meshy Marketing Team directly.
